Do I have to have an account to buy a book?

Yes! In order to give our customers the best shopping experience we require all of our users to sign up for an account.

Can I return my book?

Yes! If you are unhappy with your purchase you may return your book within 1 week for a full refund.

What if I want to find a book I previously purchased?

By going into your account and clicking on order history you will be able to see all previous orders you have made.

Do I have to signup for promotions to us the code?

No! The promotions on our website can be used by anybody, but those who sign up for our promotional emails get exclusive deals.

What does featured books mean?

Books under out featured section are the books that are most popular at the moment.

Am I able to change my payments/shipping address?

All of our registered users are able to edit any information besides their email within the my account page.

Have any more questions?

Contact us by email or by phone 24/7 and we are happy to help!
